The Overnight Revolution

Canberra author Peter DeMoor publishes The Overnight Revolution: A New Way to Get Our Days Back, a near-future travel book imagining how autonomous overnight vehicles could reshape the Australian experience of distance, time, and regional travel.

The book follows thirty mornings around Australia and asks a simple question: what if the journey happened while you slept?
