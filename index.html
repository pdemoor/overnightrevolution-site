About the book

Australia is big. Your days shouldn’t be.

The Overnight Revolution imagines a near future where fully autonomous overnight vehicles transform the way we travel.

Instead of losing weekends to driving or flying, you leave after dinner, sleep while you travel, and wake to a new Australian sunrise. Across thirty unforgettable mornings, the book explores how overnight autonomous travel could change tourism, regional communities, family time, business travel, and the way Australians experience distance.

The future of travel isn’t just about getting there faster. It’s about getting our days back.

About Peter

Peter DeMoor

Peter DeMoor is a Canberra-based public servant, podcast host, TEDx Canberra speaker, former hotel manager, former lecturer in China, and founder of Refresh.

After experiencing autonomous vehicle travel in San Francisco, he became convinced it would transform more than transport. It would change how we experience time itself.
